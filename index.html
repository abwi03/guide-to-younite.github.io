A Beginner's Guide to YOUNITE

About the Group

YOUNITE (유나이트) is a boy group under the entertainment company Brand New Music. They debuted on April 20, 2022, with their first EP album, YOUNI-BIRTH.

The group consists of 9 members: Eunho, Steve, Eunsang, Hyunseung, Hyungseok, Woono, Dey, Kyungmun, and Sion.

However, since July 1st, 2024, Hyunseung has left due to personal reasons, and YOUNITE has since continued as an 8-member group.
